How it works

📄

Drop a PDF and paste your job description. Choose from 15+ curated personas — YC Partner, Google L6 EM, Seed-stage VC — or write your own prompt in plain English.

Privacy & Data Handling

What we collect

  • Your email address and a bcrypt-hashed password when you create an account.
  • The text extracted from CV PDFs you upload, along with evaluation results (score, critique, verdict), stored against your account for your history.

What we do NOT store

  • Raw PDF files are never stored on disk. They are read into memory, processed, and discarded.
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

Third-party services

  • CV text and job description are sent to your configured LLM provider for evaluation — GitHub Models (default), OpenAI, or Anthropic, depending on the MODEL_PROVIDER setting. Subject to the privacy policy of the active provider.
  • The application is hosted on Railway.app.

Candidate data

You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to upload and process candidate CVs under applicable data protection law (e.g. GDPR).

Data retention

Evaluation history is retained until you erase it. Use History → Purge history to delete all evaluation records while keeping your account, or Account → Delete account to permanently erase all your data including your account. Both actions are irreversible.
