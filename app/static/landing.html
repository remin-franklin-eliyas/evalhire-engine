Privacy & Data Handling

What we collect

  • Your email address and a bcrypt-hashed password when you create an account.
  • The text extracted from CV PDFs you upload, along with evaluation results (score, critique, verdict), stored against your account for your history.

What we do NOT store

  • Raw PDF files are never stored on disk. They are read into memory, processed, and discarded.
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

Third-party services

  • CV text and job description are sent to GitHub Models (Microsoft Azure inference) for evaluation. Subject to GitHub's privacy policy.
  • The application is hosted on Railway.app.

Candidate data

You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to upload and process candidate CVs under applicable data protection law (e.g. GDPR).

Data retention

Evaluation history is retained until you delete your account. Contact us to request deletion.
